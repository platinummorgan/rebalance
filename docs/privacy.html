Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

We Collect NO Personal Data

Rebalance ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect absolutely no personal data.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All your financial data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud storage.
  • Encryption: Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption on your device.
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not share data with any third parties.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you create an account, all data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Device information: May include device model, OS version, and app version for crash reporting only.
  • Usage analytics: We may collect anonymous usage metrics and crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics to improve the app.

How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain the app functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve features
  • To respond to support requests

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except for third-party services that help us deliver the app (for example, Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting). We do not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You can opt out of analytics and clear local data via the app settings. If you wish to delete your data or contact us, email mdorminey79@gmail.com.

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Since we collect no personal data and store everything locally on your device, we are inherently compliant with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
